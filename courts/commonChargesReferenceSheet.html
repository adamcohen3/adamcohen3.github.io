Common Charges Reference Sheet

Judges, lawyers, and law enforcement officers often describe offenses with legal jargon. For example, in Hawaii, speeding is sometimes referred to by its section number in the legal code, HRS 291C-102, or by its title/description in the legal code, "NONCOMPLIANCE WITH SPEED LIMIT."

If you have a ticket, it should show the section number and legal description for each offense. It is important to input the offense as it is written on your ticket. If you still cannot find your offense(s) in the calculator, please contact us.

If you are a member of the public who is curious about fines and fees, the list below is designed to help you look up common offenses. It includes non-legal terms for common offenses along with their section numbers and descriptions. If the non-legal term is not recognized by the calculator, try the section number or description.

This list does not include all possible charge codes, only some of the most common.
