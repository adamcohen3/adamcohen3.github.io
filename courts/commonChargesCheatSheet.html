Common Charges Cheat Sheet

Judges, lawyers, and law enforcement often refer to offenses by their section number or title in the legal code. For example, in Hawaii, speeding falls under HRS section 291C-102, titled "NONCOMPLIANCE WITH SPEED LIMIT."

The legal code can be confusing even for people familiar with it, but it is more precise than using some of our everyday terms for offenses.

To help bridge the gap between the more familiar terms we use in everyday conversation and the technical legal terms used in the courts, and importantly to help you select the correct offense when using the calculator, refer to the cheat sheet below, which includes a sample of offenses with their HRS section numbers and titles. It is important to type in the charges as they appear on your ticket to ensure you are selecting the correct offenses.
