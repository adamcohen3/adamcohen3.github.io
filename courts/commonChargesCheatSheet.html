Common Charges Cheat Sheet

Offenses are often referred to by their HRS section numbers or titles. For example, speeding falls under HRS section 291C-102, titled "NONCOMPLIANCE WITH SPEED LIMIT."

To help select the correct offense, refer to the cheat sheet below, which includes a sample of offenses with their HRS section numbers and titles. To reduce the chances of selecting an incorrect offense, type in the charge(s) as they appear on your ticket.
